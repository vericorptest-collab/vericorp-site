FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can I mix plans across products?

Yes, each product has its own subscription on RapidAPI. You can have the Free tier on Company Verify and Pro on Invoice Extract, for example.

How does the free tier work?

Sign up on RapidAPI and start making requests immediately. No credit card required. You get 25 invoice extractions or 200 company lookups per month for free.

What happens when I hit the limit?

You'll receive a 429 error with a clear message and a Retry-After header. Upgrade your plan to continue, or wait for the next billing cycle.

Do you charge per page or per API call?

Per API call. Multi-page PDFs count as 1 extraction. A 10-page invoice is the same price as a 1-page invoice.

Is there a pay-as-you-go option?

Not yet. We're planning pay-as-you-go pricing for later in 2026. For now, choose the plan that fits your volume.
